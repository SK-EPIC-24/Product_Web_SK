About the Teacher

Disciple of Renowned Odissi Nritya Guru Shrimati Jhelum Paranjape and Grand student of Guru Kelucharan Mahapatra. Stood first all over Maharashtra in the Nritya Visharad exams from Gandharva Mahavidyalay. Masters in Odissi Nritya from Mumbai university. Acted, Choreographed and Directed many plays on various theatrical platforms all over India since 17 years.

The student learns to represent the glorious culture of bhakti, through the dance meant for the pleasure of Lord Jagannath.

Why should I learn Odissi?

As there are rigorous body conditioning exercises and postures which are extracted directly from the ancient Bharatiya scripture of Dance and Drama, The Natya Shastra. Hence, they accomplish physical fitness and grace. Having acquired various SKILLS / TALENTS like, Angik Abhinay (body movements), Vacik Abhinay (Speech expression), Aharya Abhinay (Costume expression), Satvik Abhinay (facial Expression through Mental Sadhana) helps to become an independent stage artist. There will be also theory classes imparting the Bhagvat wisdom.

A world of entertainment. Available wherever you are.

Taking online classes offers several advantages, including flexibility in learning time and location, accessibility to a wider range of courses, the ability to study at your own pace, potential cost savings, and the development of self-discipline and time management skills
